Base AC of Leather Armor: 11
Dexterity Modifier: +3
AC = 11 + 3 = 14


Impact on Armor Statistics
1. Chain Mail (Heavy Armor):------
Base AC: 16

Weight: 65 lbs

Stealth Disadvantage: Yes

Strength Requirement: 13 Strength

Proficient Character:

Uses base AC of 16.
Dexterity modifier does not affect AC.
Can wear it effectively and receive the full AC benefit.
Not Proficient Character:

Uses base AC of 16.
Cannot apply Dexterity modifier.
Suffers disadvantage on Stealth checks.
May find it cumbersome due to lack of proficiency.

2. Leather Armor (Light Armor):-----
Base AC: 11

Weight: 10 lbs

Stealth Disadvantage: No

Proficient Character:

Base AC of 11 + full Dexterity modifier.
Example: Dexterity modifier +3 results in an AC of 11 + 3 = 14.
Not Proficient Character:

Base AC of 11 + full Dexterity modifier.
Example: Dexterity modifier +3 results in an AC of 11 + 3 = 14.
May still use the armor effectively but with a general disadvantage in terms of class features.
